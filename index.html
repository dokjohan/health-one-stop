📖 Health Conditions Guide

Plain-language info. Visit your nearest clinic — applies to all patients including those without medical aid.

❤️ Hypertension (High Blood Pressure)▼
High blood pressure means your heart works too hard. You usually have NO symptoms — called the "silent killer."

⚠️ Emergency: Severe headache, blurred vision, chest pain → go immediately.

Get BP checked free at any pharmacy. Never stop medication without your doctor.
🍬 Diabetes Type 2▼
Blood sugar builds up and damages the body if not treated.

⚠️ Warning: Very thirsty, urinating a lot, blurred vision, slow-healing wounds, tingling feet.

Never stop medication without your doctor. Wound that won't heal → go to clinic immediately.
💨 Asthma▼
Airways narrow, making it hard to breathe — especially with exercise, cold air, dust, smoke.

🚨 Emergency: Reliever inhaler (blue pump) not helping after 10 minutes → call 10177.
🎗️ HIV / ARTs▼
ARTs (antiretroviral treatment) control the virus. ARTs are FREE at all SA public clinics.

Take every day at the same time. Never miss doses. Undetectable viral load = cannot pass HIV to others (U=U).
🫁 Tuberculosis (TB)▼
TB is curable with 6 months of treatment. Never stop early.

⚠️ Symptoms: Cough 2+ weeks, coughing blood, night sweats, weight loss, fatigue → go to clinic immediately. Testing is FREE.
